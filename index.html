The Dominican American Coalition of Connecticut

What does The Dominican American Coalition of Connecticut do?

Through the execution of human development programs, The Dominican American Coalition of Connecticut works for the benefit of the inhabitants of the State of Connecticut by applying models of social intervention that seek the transformation of people, applying the "Value-Art" method.

Educating communities in:

  • Handicrafts.
  • Financial education.
  • Metabolism and nutrition.
  • Basic computer literacy for adults.
  • ESL program.
  • Musical Instruments.
  • Sports programs.
